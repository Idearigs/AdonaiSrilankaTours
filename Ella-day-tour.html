Ella Day tour

Hiking, Scenic Train Rides, and Iconic Ella Views.

  • 1D
  • Category : Honeymoon tours

OVERVIEW :

Escape to the hill country on this adventure-filled day tour in Ella. Start with a visit to the breathtaking Nine Arches Bridge, an iconic feat of architecture amidst lush surroundings. Embark on a hike up either Ella Rock or Little Adam's Peak, each offering spectacular views over the tea-covered hills. Complete your Ella experience with a scenic train ride through tea plantations and verdant landscapes, giving you a glimpse of Sri Lanka’s famous tea country. The Ella Adventure Tour is a perfect day for outdoor enthusiasts and those in search of Sri Lanka’s natural beauty.

INCLUDE & EXCLUDE :

  • Comfortable hotel accommodations
  • Entry fees to all cultural sites
  • Daily breakfast and select lunches
  • Air-conditioned transportation
  • Professional English-speaking guide
