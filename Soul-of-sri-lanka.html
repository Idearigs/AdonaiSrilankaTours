Soul of sri lanka

Thrilling rafting, scenic hikes, and tranquil tea hills.

  • 7D/6N
  • Category : Culture tours

OVERVIEW :

The Adventure and Nature Exploration Package offers an ideal blend of thrill and tranquility, designed for adventurous souls and nature lovers alike. Begin your journey with a warm welcome in Colombo, where a city tour introduces you to key sights like Independence Square and the vibrant Pettah market, offering an authentic taste of local life.

Your adventure kicks off in Kitulgala, Sri Lanka’s go-to destination for adrenaline-packed experiences. Feel the rush as you navigate the rapids of the Kelani River on a white-water rafting expedition or enjoy a jungle trek, immersing yourself in lush, tropical scenery.

Next, you’ll ascend to the cool highlands of Nuwara Eliya, a charming town renowned for its scenic tea estates. Enjoy a visit to a tea plantation and factory, complete with a tasting session of freshly brewed Ceylon tea. An evening stroll around the serene Gregory Lake completes your day in this "Little England" of Sri Lanka.

On day four, set out early to explore Horton Plains National Park, embarking on a hike to World’s End, where breathtaking views await at the cliff’s edge. A scenic train ride to Ella the following day reveals stunning landscapes, leading to a relaxed day in Ella’s cozy cafes and renowned tea fields.

From Ella, a day trip to Haputale offers remarkable viewpoints like Lipton’s Seat. Your journey back to Colombo includes a stop at a spice garden in Matale, ending your adventure with aromatic flavors.

INCLUDE & EXCLUDE :

  • Comfortable hotel accommodations
  • Entry fees to all cultural sites
  • Daily breakfast and select lunches
  • Air-conditioned transportation
  • Professional English-speaking guide

ITINERARY :

This tour offers a thrilling mix of white-water rafting, scenic train rides, and serene tea plantation visits, perfect for nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers exploring Sri Lanka’s diverse landscapes.

  • DAY 1 Arrival in Colombo, city tour.
  • DAY 2 Transfer to Kitulgala, white-water rafting or jungle trek.
  • DAY 3 Travel to Nuwara Eliya, tea plantation visit, evening at Gregory Lake.
  • DAY 4 Early hike to World’s End in Horton Plains.
  • DAY 5 Scenic train ride to Ella, explore tea fields.
  • DAY 6 Day trip to Haputale, visit Lipton’s Seat.
  • DAY 7 Return to Colombo, visit a spice garden in Matale, and depart.
