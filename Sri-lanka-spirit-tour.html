Sri lanka spirit tour

Explore Sri Lanka’s cultural treasures, landscapes, and wildlife.

  • 7D/6N
  • Category : Culture tours

OVERVIEW :

The Cultural and Scenic Highlights Package, part of our Summer Tours collection, offers an immersive journey through Sri Lanka’s vibrant culture, iconic landmarks, and breathtaking natural beauty. Begin in Colombo, where you’ll explore the city’s bustling markets, the National Museum, and the oceanside expanse of Galle Face Green.

From Colombo, you’ll travel to Sigiriya to experience the awe-inspiring Sigiriya Rock Fortress, a UNESCO World Heritage site known for its remarkable rock carvings, lush gardens, and panoramic views. Your next stop is the historic city of Polonnaruwa en route to Kandy, where ancient temples, statues, and ruins await. In Kandy, enjoy a mesmerizing cultural show and visit the revered Temple of the Tooth, one of Buddhism’s most significant sites.

The journey continues with a scenic drive to Nuwara Eliya, a charming highland town famous for tea plantations and colonial architecture. From here, take a picturesque train ride to Ella, marveling at the verdant landscapes along the way. In Ella, you’ll visit the famous Nine Arches Bridge and hike to Ella Rock or Little Adam's Peak for stunning views.

An adventure awaits in Yala National Park, where a thrilling safari offers sightings of elephants, leopards, and a variety of wildlife. Finally, explore the coastal charm of Galle, visiting the historic Galle Fort and relaxing by the beach before returning to Colombo.

INCLUDE & EXCLUDE :

  • Comfortable hotel accommodations
  • Entry fees to all cultural sites
  • Daily breakfast and select lunches
  • Air-conditioned transportation
  • Professional English-speaking guide

ITINERARY :

This tour blends cultural heritage, scenic train rides, and wildlife safaris, taking you from Colombo to Sigiriya, Kandy, Nuwara Eliya, Ella, Yala, and Galle for a complete Sri Lankan experience.

  • DAY 1 Arrival in Colombo. City exploration.
  • DAY 2 Transfer to Sigiriya, visit Sigiriya Rock Fortress.
  • DAY 3 En route to Kandy, explore Polonnaruwa. Evening cultural show.
  • DAY 4 Visit Royal Botanical Gardens, transfer to Nuwara Eliya.
  • DAY 5 Scenic train ride to Ella, visit Nine Arches Bridge.
  • DAY 6 Travel to Yala, afternoon safari.
  • DAY 7 Explore Galle Fort, relax at the beach, return to Colombo for departure.
