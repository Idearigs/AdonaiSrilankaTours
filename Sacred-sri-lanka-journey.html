Sacred sri lanka journey

Uncover Sri Lanka’s spiritual and ancient heritage sites.

  • 7D/6N
  • Category : Culture tours

OVERVIEW :

Embark on a profound journey through Sri Lanka’s spiritual and cultural wonders with the Cultural and Spiritual Journey package. This tour is designed for travelers who seek not only to see the beauty of Sri Lanka but also to experience its ancient spirituality and cultural richness firsthand. Start in Colombo, exploring sacred temples like Gangaramaya and the historic Old Dutch Hospital.

The journey continues to Kandy, where the sacred Temple of the Tooth Relic invites you to participate in an early morning prayer ceremony, setting the tone for a peaceful and insightful day. After enjoying a Kandyan dance performance, move toward Dambulla, home to the stunning Dambulla Cave Temples with elaborate murals and Buddha statues, and a chance to visit a spice garden in Matale.

The journey continues to Sigiriya, where you’ll scale the iconic Sigiriya Rock Fortress, a marvel of ancient engineering and artistry, with panoramic views from the summit. A local village experience offers insight into rural life and traditional Sri Lankan cuisine.

In Sigiriya, climb the Sigiriya Rock Fortress and take in panoramic views, while nearby Pidurangala Rock offers an alternative vantage point. Explore Anuradhapura’s ancient relics, including the iconic Ruwanwelisaya stupa, and experience the spiritual essence of this historic city.

At Mihintale, believed to be the birthplace of Buddhism in Sri Lanka, you’ll hike to ancient stupas and monastic buildings, culminating in breathtaking views from the summit. Conclude your journey back in Colombo with time to shop and relax by the beach.

INCLUDE & EXCLUDE :

  • Comfortable hotel accommodations
  • Entry fees to all cultural sites
  • Daily breakfast and select lunches
  • Air-conditioned transportation
  • Professional English-speaking guide

ITINERARY :

Immerse yourself in Sri Lanka’s spiritual landmarks, from sacred temples and cave sanctuaries to UNESCO World Heritage sites, experiencing the island’s serene beauty and profound cultural roots.

  • DAY 1 Arrival in Colombo, city highlights tour. Arrival in Colombo, cultural landmarks tour.
  • DAY 2 Transfer to Kandy, Temple of the Tooth, cultural dance.
  • DAY 3 Journey to Dambulla, explore cave temples, visit spice garden.
  • DAY 4 Sigiriya Rock Fortress and Pidurangala Rock.
  • DAY 5 Explore Anuradhapura, Ruwanwelisaya stupa, Samadhi Buddha.
  • DAY 6 Visit Mihintale, hike to ancient stupas.
  • DAY 7 Return to Colombo, local market shopping, beach relaxation.
